## Register an Account

Head over to the [Account Registration](https://scan.coredao.org/register) page and provide username, email and password for your account.

A confirmation link ğŸ”— will be sent to your email address to verify your sign up request. Once you've clicked on the link, your account set-up process is complete and you may start to use your account-specific features ! ğŸ‰
